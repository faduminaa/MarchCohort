Quick and easy almond and date smoothie bowl for suhuur

Get ready to make a tasty ramadan smoothie bowl

    Ingredients:
  • 200 milk of or other milk alternative
  • 4 large soft dates
  • 10 - 12 almonds
  • 1/2 cup of greek yoghurt
    Steps:
  1. In a blender add milk, almonds and dates
  2. Blend for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy
  3. To make the liquid into a thick consistency add in the greek yoghurt
  4. The recipe calls for 1/2 cup of greek yoghurt but you can add more or less depending on your preference
  5. pour into a bowl, add toppings of your desire and enjoy!
